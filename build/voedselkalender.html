Voedselkalender (food calendar)

Voedselkalender is a (Dutch and French) Windows Phone app that keeps it simple. It shows a list of vegetables or fruit that are seasonal for the current month. Tapping on an item shows the relevant Wikipedia page.

Voedselkalender has an average of 4.5 out of 5 stars.
